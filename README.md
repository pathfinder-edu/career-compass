# Career Compass

A free, friendly AI guide that helps a student finishing high school figure out what to study or what career to explore.

It is just a prompt — no app, no sign-up, no install. Paste it into any AI chat (ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, Copilot) and have a calm 15-20 minute conversation. It ends with 3-4 concrete career or course directions and a small first step to test each one.

## Who it is for

- Students who are not sure what to pick — and that is completely okay.
- Parents who want to help without pressure.
- Teachers and school counselors who want a simple conversation starter.

## How to use

1. Open [`career-compass.md`](career-compass.md).
2. Copy everything below the line that says `PASTE FROM HERE`.
3. Open any AI chat and paste it as your first message.
4. Say "hi" and talk honestly. There are no wrong answers.

Full instructions are at the top of [`career-compass.md`](career-compass.md).

## Why it works this way

- **Model-agnostic** — works on any AI chat, because it is plain text.
- **One question at a time** — a conversation, not an interrogation.
- **No prestige push** — trades, gap years, apprenticeships, and degrees are all treated as equally valid.
- **Region-aware** — it asks the student's country so course advice fits where they live.
- **Honest** — every suggested path comes with a real reality check.

## A note

Career Compass is a starting compass, not a final answer. It does not replace a real career counselor or the student's own research. If a student seems to be struggling emotionally, please encourage them to talk to a trusted adult or a professional.
